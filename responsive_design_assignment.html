Check Out This New Cafe in Milwaukee!

The Daily Bird

Read about some of Milwaukee's best cafe's here:

Rochambo Coffee & Tea House

A community staple on Brady street known for its "funky" vibe and delicious coffee made with locally roasted organic beans. The customer service is better than any cafe I've visited. The barista's are friendly and will happily reccommend you a drink to your liking. You can tell their drinks are made with care and the coffee is always fresh.

Blum Coffee Garden

A relaxing place to study and enjoy your favorite Collectivo drink and pastry. The study space is surrounded by plants and it feels like your still outside. You can also take a study break and walk around their garden, whenever you like.

Stone Creek Coffee

This is a chic local cafe chain offering coffee, tea, and bakery items. Their menu is filled with surprises, including a Mexican Spice Mocha and Ube Latte. The best part is, they have a location a couple blocks from campus!
